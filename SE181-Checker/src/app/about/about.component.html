What is checkers?

Checkers is a game about the story of rising through the ranks and capturing your enemies.

How to play?

The objective of the game is to capture all your opponent's pieces or trap their pieces.

The game alternates turns between 2 players. Black moves first. During your turn, move your pieces in any forward diagonal direction to any empty square.

If an opponent piece is blocking your move, if there's an empty space diagonal behind them, you may "jump" over them, landing on said empty square in one swift direction, removing their piece from the game and moving towards the empty piece behind them.

When you move your piece to the end of the board, you can promote that piece into a King. Being a king enables that piece to move backwards diagonally in addition to forwards.

You win by capturing all the opponent's pieces or trapping their pieces such that they can't move.
